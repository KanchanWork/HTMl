The Algarve, Portugal

While there is no denying that winter sea swimming is reserved for the fearless,
there is still some good surf and sun-kissed days to be had.
With over 300 sunny days a year and heavily discounted prices outside summer,
the off-season in the Algarve is a good gamble to get your winter Vitamin C.
